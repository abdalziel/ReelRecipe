## 1. Introduction

ReelRecipe ("we," "us," or "our") is a mobile and web application that helps users save, organize, and plan meals from cooking video content. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use ReelRecipe ("the Service").

By creating an account or using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the Service.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide the Service** — operate your recipe library, meal planner, and shopping lists
2. **Extract recipes** — your uploaded video or reel transcript and caption are sent to Anthropic's Claude AI API to identify and structure recipe information (see Section 5)
3. **Enforce subscription limits** — track daily import counts and library size per your tier
4. **Process payments** — manage your subscription via the relevant payment provider
5. **Send transactional emails** — account confirmation, password reset, subscription receipts
6. **Improve the Service** — aggregate, anonymized analytics on feature usage
7. **Ensure security** — detect fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
8. **Comply with legal obligations** — respond to lawful requests from authorities

We do **not** use your personal data for advertising. We do **not** sell your data to third parties.

## 4. Diet and Health Data

## 7. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

- **Encryption in transit** — all communications use HTTPS/TLS 1.2+
- **Encryption at rest** — database (RDS) and file storage (S3) are encrypted using AES-256 via AWS Key Management Service (KMS)
- **Password hashing** — passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage
- **Secrets management** — API keys and credentials are stored in AWS Secrets Manager, not in code or environment files
- **Access controls** — production database is not publicly accessible; accessed only from application servers within a private VPC
- **Instagram credentials** — never persisted (see Section 2.5)

Despite these measures, no system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by applicable law within 72 hours.

## 10. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from **Settings → Account → Delete Account** within the app.

Upon deletion:
- Your email, recipes, meal plans, diet goals, and shopping lists are permanently deleted within **30 days**
- Thumbnails and uploaded files stored in S3 are deleted within **30 days**
- Anonymized aggregate data (not linked to your identity) may be retained
- Billing records required by law (7 years) are retained in compliance with tax regulations but are isolated from your profile

This process is irreversible. If you wish to retain your recipes before deleting, use the data export feature first.
